About X1 USA

X1 USA was born with the mission to transform the 1v1 soccer scene in the United States. Inspired by a global movement that values individuality, skill, and creativity on the field, X1 promotes exciting tournaments, official licensing, and initiatives that foster the growth of this innovative sport.

As a proud Non-Profit Organization 501(c)(3), we unite athletes, fans, and brands in authentic, electrifying, and purpose-driven sporting experiences.
